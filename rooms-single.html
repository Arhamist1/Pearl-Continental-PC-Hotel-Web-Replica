    WELCOME TO PC LEGACY NASIRABAD HUNZA

    Overlooking Rakaposhi, PC Legacy Nasirabad, Hunza combines a total of 57 rooms, including deluxe and family rooms, as well as suites, extending the best services and comfort to all travellers. Majority of the rooms come with balconies opening to the scenic views of the Rakaposhi. The Rakaposhi mountain range is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, rising majestically in the northern regions of Pakistan. The range is characterized by its towering peaks, draped in snow and ice, that seem to touch the sky. The slopes are lush and verdant, dotted with wildflowers that bloom in the spring. The view from the mountain range is simply breathtaking, offering panoramic vistas of the surrounding valleys, rivers, and forests. Whether viewed from a distance or from its summit, the Rakaposhi mountain range is a true masterpiece of nature, inspiring awe and wonder in all who behold it.
